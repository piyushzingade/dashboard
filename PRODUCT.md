# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers, product teams, and SaaS operators use NexUI as a production-ready dashboard foundation. They need to scan business health, manage products and transactions, plan work, and configure an account without relearning the interface on every screen.

## Product Purpose

NexUI demonstrates and ships a complete, customizable dashboard system for Next.js. Success means dense operational information remains fast to scan, every workflow feels coherent, and teams can adapt the theme without breaking accessibility or component consistency.

## Brand Personality

Precise, assured, and composed. The product should feel like a carefully calibrated instrument: quiet at rest, explicit in state, and trustworthy under sustained daily use.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic soft SaaS cards, colorful decorative gradients, glassmorphism, oversized radii, low-contrast gray-on-gray text, ornamental motion, and dashboards that scatter unrelated accent colors across every chart. The interface should not feel toy-like, padded-out, or assembled from mismatched component demos.

## Design Principles

1. Make hierarchy measurable: the most important number, action, and state must be identifiable at a glance.
2. Use density with discipline: compact layouts are welcome when grouping, alignment, and typography keep them legible.
3. Let state carry color: neutrals define structure; green, red, amber, and the selected theme accent communicate meaning.
4. Practice component continuity: the same control behaves and reads the same way on every route.
5. Design for long sessions: reduce visual noise, avoid layout shift, and keep keyboard and touch workflows equally complete.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, semantic landmarks and labels, 44px touch targets, color-independent status communication, color-blind-safe chart differentiation, and complete `prefers-reduced-motion` behavior. Text and controls must retain sufficient contrast in every bundled light and dark theme.
